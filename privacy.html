Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2024

PulseNova Technology Limited ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile applications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, input health metrics (e.g., age, height, weight, body fat), or contact support. Because our app deals with health and fitness data, we prioritize local device storage and secure cloud syncing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including calculating personalized fitness goals, daily calorie targets, and tracking your physical progress.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is protected using industry-standard security measures.

4. Account Deletion and Data Retention

You have the right to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time. You can do this directly within the app by navigating to the Settings menu and selecting "Delete Account". Upon confirming deletion, all your personal data will be permanently and irreversibly removed from our active servers.
